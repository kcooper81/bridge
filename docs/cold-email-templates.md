## Usage Notes

- **Recommended:** Start with Option A — subject line is attention-grabbing, leads with a concrete visual, low-friction CTA.
- Use the others as follow-up emails spaced 3-4 days apart.
- Keep under 100 words per email.
- No attachments or links in the first email (triggers spam filters).
- Personalize the first line when possible (reference their company, role, or a recent initiative).
